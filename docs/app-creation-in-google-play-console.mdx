<Frame caption="Create Google Play App">
  <img src="/images/docs/655b86d-Screenshot_createapp.png" />
</Frame>

Fill in the details required and click on **Create app**.

![](/images/docs/5283626-Screenshot_app_details.png)

**2. Set up the app** You have to complete 9 steps to set up your app.

![](/images/docs/346d8ed-Screenshot_1.png)

**2.1 App access** If your application has any forms of authentication, you have to provide the access details.

![](/images/docs/3214a2c-Screenshot_app_access.png)

**2.2 Content ratings** Complete the questionnaire to receive your content ratings for the app.

![](/images/docs/7479d4e-Screenshot_content_raiting.png)

**2.3 Target audience and content** Manage [target audience and app content](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9867159) settings.

![](/images/docs/dabb466-Screenshot_targer_audience.png)

**2.4 News app** Answer the question about the use of ads in your application.

![](/images/docs/dc3b53a-Screenshot_news_app.png)

**2.5 App category** Select your app category and provide your contact details.

![](/images/docs/97e1fb8-Screenshot_app_category.png)

**2.6 Set up the store listing** Fill in the app's name and description.

![](/images/docs/d7d9ebb-Screenshot_store_listing.png)

Provide the app icon and screenshots that will be shown on Google Play.

![](/images/docs/248c306-Screenshot_1.png) ![](/images/docs/08e9a47-Screenshot_screen.png)

**2.7 Merchant account** If you don't have a merchant account yet, [create a payments profile](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/7161426?hl=en).

**2.8 Set the price for your app**

![](/images/docs/31561c4-Screenshot_App_pricing.png)

<Warning>
  If you choose to offer your app for free, you can not change that in the future.
</Warning>
