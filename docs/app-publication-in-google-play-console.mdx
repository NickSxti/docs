---
title: "Publish your Android App"
description: "If you want to test in-app purchases, your app must be published (either production, alpha or beta channels, )."
---

**1. Create closed track** Navigate to [Google Play Developer Console](https://play.google.com/apps/publish/) and select the 'All apps' tab. Then choose your app from the list. Select the **Testing->Closed Testing** tab in the left menu bar. Select Alpha from the Active tracks list and click **Manage track** opposite it.

![1853](/images/docs/557a96f-Screenshot_10.png "Screenshot_10.png")

Click on the **Create new release** button.

![](/images/docs/973b6e5-Screenshot_11.png)

**2. Upload the signed .apk file** Upload the [signed](https://developer.android.com/studio/publish/app-signing#sign-apk) App Bundle or APK file.

![1562](/images/docs/ea0deec-Screenshot_12.png "Screenshot_12.png")

After uploading the file, save the release.

![1574](/images/docs/94a40b5-Screenshot_13.png "Screenshot_13.png")

**3. Add countries/regions for test** You can target your Beta track release to users in [specific countries](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/7550024?hl=en). Add available countries to test the track.

![1169](/images/docs/22b010c-Screenshot_15.png "Screenshot_15.png")

**4. Configure a list of testers (Optional)** If you want your release to be available to certain users on Google Play, but not visible to anyone else, please specify a \[list of testers] ([https://play.google.com/console/about/closed-testing/](https://play.google.com/console/about/closed-testing/)). Click the **Create email list** button.

![1084](/images/docs/74bc36f-Screenshot_email_list.png "Screenshot_email_list.png")

Add email address and click **Save changes**.

![949](/images/docs/a49a243-Screenshot_testers.png "Screenshot_testers.png")

Аfter the application is published, testers will be able to join your test.

![722](/images/docs/c913953-Screenshot_tester_link.png "Screenshot_tester_link.png")

**5. Roll out** The release is currently in Draft status. Click the **Edit** button.

![438](/images/docs/8027856-Screenshot_edit_release.png "Screenshot_edit_release.png")

Click the **Review release** button to check the release.

![399](/images/docs/ecc3d0d-Screenshot_review_release.png "Screenshot_review_release.png")

If the release is ready, click the **Start rollout to Alpha** button.

![371](/images/docs/2598128-Screenshot_start_rollout.png "Screenshot_start_rollout.png")

<Warning>
  ### Before making a purchase make sure your release is approved and available to selected testers.
</Warning>

![352](/images/docs/da79384-Screenshot_active_release.png "Screenshot_active_release.png")

<Info>
  ### Publishing time

  When you publish your application for the first time, it may take up to several days. Later releases usually take several hours.
</Info>

<Warning>
  ### Make sure that the uploaded App Bundle or APK file matches the one you are testing when it comes to version code, version name, applicationId and keystore signature.
</Warning>
