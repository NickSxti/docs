## Customization

After adding a Feature List template, you can customize:

**Content:**

* Feature titles and descriptions
* Icon selection (from Tabler icon library)
* Emoji choices
* Number of features displayed

**Styling:**

* Icon colors and sizes
* Typography (font, size, weight)
* Spacing and alignment
* Background colors
* Divider styles

***

## Best Practices

* **Keep it scannable** — 3-5 features is optimal for mobile
* **Lead with value** — put your strongest benefits first
* **Use consistent icons** — stick to one icon style throughout
* **Match your brand** — use brand colors for icons and accents
* **Be specific** — "Unlimited exports" beats "More features"
* **Highlight differences** — in comparisons, emphasize what's exclusive to premium
* **Test readability** — ensure text is legible on all device sizes
