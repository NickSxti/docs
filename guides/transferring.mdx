---
title: "Restore behavior"
description: "Two available behaviors exist for reassigning the same subscription to different users:"
---

* Transfer entitlement: Entitlement is to be transferred to another Identity user on restore.
* Copy entitlement: Entitlement is to be copied to another Identity user on restore.

For configuring this feature, navigate to the [entitlements settings section](https://dash.qonversion.io/entitlements/transferring).
